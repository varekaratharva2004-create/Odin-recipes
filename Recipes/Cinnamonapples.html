Cinnamon Apples

These soft and warm cinnamon apples are easy to make in the microwave. My family loves them for dessert with ice cream or side dish with pork chops.

This recipe creates the perfect warm, tender cinnamon apples in minutes using just a microwave. Home cooks like customizing sweetness and texture to fit their needs and preferences.

Ingredients

  • 2 medium apples, diced
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar, or to taste
  • Â½ teaspoon ground cinnamon

Insructions

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Place apples in a microwave-safe bowl; cook in the microwave for 30 seconds. Sprinkle sugar and cinnamon over apples and stir to coat.
  3. Continue cooking apples in the microwave until soft and warm, about 1 minute more.
